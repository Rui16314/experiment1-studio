You will play 10 rounds of a first‑price sealed‑bid auction. Each round you are randomly matched with an opponent.

  • Your valuation is drawn uniformly from 0 to 100 (two decimals).
  • You have 60 seconds to submit a bid between 0 and 100.
  • Highest bid wins and pays their own bid (first‑price). Ties are broken at random.
  • Payoff if you win: valuation − price; otherwise 0.
